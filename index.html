My childhood

Starting fom the scratch I was born in the capital of Ethiopia(specifically in Tikur Anbessa Hospital) in May 22 2000(in Gregorian calander). Then my family moved to Hawassa for living. Although I cannot remember my childhod Hawwasa is my beloved city from my faded meomeries. After 6 years we moved back to Addis Ababa. Then we started living in our current residence (Asko in Gulele subcity).

Education

I learned pre-school in Ascko Progress Acadamy. I finished primary school in Asko Progress Acadamy ( from grade 1 up to grade 6) and Lideta Catholic Cathedral School( from grade 7 up to grade 8). I finished secondary school in Lideta Catholic Catedral School. Most of my memories about my life are connected o my school life (specially in LCCS). As every child in generation I always had diffrent answers when people asked me what I want to be when I grow up. I didnot get the question untill I was grade 5 student. My answer was to be a Pilot in grade 7. In grade 9 I wanted to be a desiner. In grade 12 I wanterd to join the medical school or be an arctect but none of this assumptions became true.

Youth

Now I am an engeneering student at ITSC center of Addis Ababa Institute of Technology. I am a first year student in Software Engineering Department of AAIT. My life at AAIT is always about attending endless classes, doing assignments, studing for tests and getting surprized by the results.
My hobbies are hiking, listning(anything), swimming, travelig etc...I prefer reading books over sport and watching movies over paying games. They are part of hobbies. And mostly I enjoy thinking about somthing in a quite and dark place.
